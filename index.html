Meta (Instagram / Facebook) — A/B Test

Built a Meta ad campaign positioning Ryze as a jitter-free coffee alternative for young professionals. Creative and copy used a problem-solution framework addressing pain points like anxiety and energy crashes. Structured A/B testing on CTA language evaluated impact on click-through and conversion performance.

Variant A — "Shop Now" · Bottom-of-funnel
Direct purchase CTA targets high-intent users ready to convert. Best suited for retargeting audiences already familiar with the brand.
Variant B — "Ryze & Grind" · Top-of-funnel
Brand-aligned lifestyle CTA builds identity resonance with new audiences. Designed to drive awareness and engagement before a purchase ask.

Google Search Ad

Developed Google Search ad mockups targeting high-intent users actively seeking alternatives to traditional coffee. Ad copy addresses key consumer pain points while positioning Ryze as the solution for calm, sustained energy.
